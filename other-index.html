Who we are

Our mission
& vision

Mission
Beacon Educational Consult develops and supplies NaCCA-aligned Schemes of Work, Lesson Notes, Examination Questions, Textbooks, and Workbooks — created by practicing teachers for practicing teachers — to improve the quality and consistency of instruction in basic schools across Ghana.
Vision
To become a leading teacher-led source of academic materials in Ghana, known for curriculum accuracy, classroom relevance, and dependable quality.
Our story

A consortium of experienced educators

Beacon Educational Consult was founded by a group of experienced Ghanaian teachers who recognised a gap in the market for high-quality, curriculum-accurate academic materials that actually reflect how teaching and learning happens in Ghanaian basic school classrooms.

What began as a conversation among colleagues has grown into a structured, teacher-led publishing and professional development partnership — united by the belief that teachers, not distant publishers, are best placed to create the materials that shape how Ghanaian children are taught.

  • Professional collaboration and resource sharing among experienced educators
  • Development and distribution of curriculum-aligned academic materials
  • Commercial sustainability through the sale of quality educational materials
  • Capacity building through workshops, seminars, and conferences for fellow educators
